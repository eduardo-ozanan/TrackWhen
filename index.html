Privacy Policy

Last updated: Aug 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using the TrackWhen mobile application ("App").

1. Your Data Stays Private

Your privacy is important. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared through this App.

The data you enter (such as birthdays, anniversaries, and any events) are stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • And automatically synced to your personal iCloud account if iCloud Drive is enabled on your device

This data is not accessible by the app developer or any third parties.

2. iCloud Sync

When iCloud Drive is enabled on your device, your data is automatically synced to your personal iCloud account. This allows you to access your data across your Apple devices using the same Apple ID.

The developer does not have access to your iCloud data. All syncing and storage are handled by Apple's iCloud services.

If you wish to delete your app data stored in iCloud:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top, then tap iCloud
  3. Tap Manage Storage or iCloud Storage
  4. Select TrackWhen
  5. Tap Delete Data to remove your synced data

3. Calendar Integration

Our app offers an optional calendar integration feature that allows you to view and import events directly into the app. This feature requires your explicit permission to access your calendar and operates in a read-only manner — it never writes to, edits, or deletes any of your calendar events. All calendar data remains stored locally on your device and is never collected, transmitted, or shared with any external server, API, or third party — not even the developer. Only you can view your calendar information within the app. Granting access is completely safe, and you can be assured that your calendar data will always remain private and secure.

4. In-App Purchases

All transactions are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We use a third-party service to manage in-app purchases. This service may collect limited, non-identifiable data, such as purchase history or device type, solely to validate transactions.

5. Your Data Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored by me, you retain full control over your information. All data is stored either locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app (removes local data)
